# Algorand JNTU Onboarding

Welcome to the official developer onboarding portal for **Algorand JNTU**. This guide is designed to take you from zero to building production-ready decentralized applications on the Algorand blockchain.

## Why Algorand?

Algorand is a high-performance, carbon-neutral blockchain that solves the "Blockchain Trilemma" of security, scalability, and decentralization. With its **Pure Proof of Stake (PPoS)** consensus, it offers:

- **Instant Finality**: Transactions are confirmed in seconds.
- **Low Fees**: Scalable infrastructure with minimal costs.
- **Powerful Logic**: Write smart contracts in Python or TypeScript.
